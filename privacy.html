Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026

This website (kierandevaney.vercel.app) is a personal, professional website for Kieran Devaney — journalist, broadcaster and author. It does not have user accounts, does not sell anything directly, and does not use a contact form, so there is very little personal data involved in visiting it. This page explains what little there is.

What this site collects

This site does not use cookies, does not run any advertising or tracking scripts, and does not collect names, email addresses, or any other personal details from visitors.

Like almost any website, basic technical logs (such as IP address, browser type, and pages requested) are automatically recorded by our hosting provider, Vercel, for security and reliability purposes. These logs are not used to identify individual visitors and are not sold or shared with third parties.

Contacting Kieran

If you email or call Kieran directly using the details on the Contact page, that information is used only to respond to your enquiry — for interviews, talks, guided tours, or press requests — and is not added to any mailing list or shared with third parties.

Links to other websites

This site links out to third-party sites — including Amazon, RTÉ, Muck Rack, IrishCentral, and Facebook — for books, press coverage, and documentaries. Once you follow one of those links, that site's own privacy policy applies, not this one.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes to the site. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Questions

For any privacy-related questions, contact kierandevaney6@gmail.com.
